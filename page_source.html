    About company

    Tecknotrove Systems designs and manufacturers worlds most Advanced Simulation Technologies for a variety of Critical Training Applications. Branded as TecknoSIM ,the company offers a range of Training Simulators for Industries like Mining, Airports , Military and Automobile. Since its inception in 2002 the company has successfully served clients in over 18 countries around the world At Tecknotrove we practice Market Driven Innovation, Being a leading player in the industry, we feel responsible and are committed to continuous innovation and R&D. Our expertise lies in our ability to apply simulation technologies - to the real-world customer needs. With an inhouse Research & Development Centre and world class manufacturing facility, we are fully equipped to cater the simulation needs of civil and military applications. We understand that your success is in getting the right solution for your problems , Whether it is improving productivity and safety, lowering operating costs or training & preparing existing personnel , we have the expertise to analyse your needs and deliver customised training solutions.
